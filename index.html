About Me

My name is Nick and I love to learn about coding and solve complex problems. Perhaps my favorite activity is to present data in entertaining and provocative ways.

I have a constant fascination with the cutting edge of technology and enjoy working on a diverse team of people moving toward a common goal.

My formal background is in law and economics, and I am naturally drawn to political news and complex discussion about an array of topics concerning how to improve the lives of people around the world.   Technology to me is a way to the end goal of improving the lives of the inhabitants of this planet.
